Recipe for Pizza

Chewy thin-crust pizzas are popular here in Portland,OR and I've been searching a long time for a recipe that would replicate restaurant crusts.
This delicious recipe will blow your socks off!

Ingredients

Sourdough Pizza Crust
Makes one 12 inch pizza

  • 1 and a half cup sourdough starter
  • 1 and a half cup bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Mix ingredients together, roll to size and place on pizza pan.
  3. Let dough rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Bake on lower-middle rack for 5 minutes.
  5. remove from oven and add your choice of base and toppings.
  6. Put back in the oven and bake for 10 more minutes untill done.
