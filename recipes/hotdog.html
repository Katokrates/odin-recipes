Hotdogs

A hot dog is a food consisting ofa grilled or steamed sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 hot dog (or as many more you need)
  • 1 hot dog bun (1 for every hotdog you make
  • Condiments you like (Ketchup, mustard, cheese or mayonaise)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan over medium-high heat, bring enough water to cover the hotdogs to a simmer.
  2. Place hot dogs in the water and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes. (After you turn off your stove you can leave them in the water to keep them warm.
  3. Place your hot dog in the sliced open bun and top with any condiments you like.
