Lasagna

Lasagna are a type of wide, flat pasta.

It is an italian dish made of stacked layers of this flat pasta alternating with fillings such as ragu and other vegetables, cheese, and spices such as garlic oregano and basil.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Tomato Sauce

  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 36 ounces of Chopped tomatoes in a can
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Cheese Mixture

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • cups fresh parsley
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 190c. Cook pasta al dente according to package directions. Rince under cold watter and set aside.
  2. Brown beef, sausage, onion and garlic over medium high heat until no pink remains. Drain any fat.
  3. Stir in pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning. Simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Make Cheese Mixture by combining mozzarella, parmesan, ricotta, parsley and egg.

  5. Add 1 cup meat sauce to a 9x13 pan.

    Top with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with a third of the Cheese Mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce.

    Repeat twice more.

    Finish with 3 noodles topped with remainig sauce.

  6. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes.

  7. Uncover, sprinkle with remaining cheese, and bake an additional 15 minutes or until browned and bubbly. Broil 2-3 minutes if desired.

  8. Rest 10-15 minutes before cutting.
